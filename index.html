Game Controls

Movement

  • W - ArrowUp:

    Forwards

  • A - ArrowLeft:

    Left

  • S - ArrowDown:

    Backwards

  • D - ArrowRight:

    Right

Interaction

  • Interact:

    Mouse click

  • ESC:

    Open Menu

Player Camera

  • Left Control:

    Toggle camera lock

  • Mouse movement:

    Rotate camera

Cube View

  • W:

    Rotate up

  • A:

    Rotate left

  • S:

    Rotate down

  • D:

    Rotate right

  • E:

    Exit
